Restaurant Developments Ltd

Restaurant digital content is a valuable resource on the internet but currently this information is fragmented and duplicated amongst various competing businesses. In practice, this endless duplication of content across multiple channels make it virtually impossible for restaurants to track and update their content and this disjointed and costly approach not only fails the restaurants, but the distributors and consumers too.

Restaurant Developments Ltd (RDL) is a new provider of restaurant content services and has developed a strategy that we believe offers a solution to these problems and that will disrupt a market, heavily biased toward large booking sites.
